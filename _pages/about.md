I am an undergraduate student at the [Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST)](https://www.kaist.ac.kr/en/), pursuing a B.S. in Electrical Engineering. During 2024–2025 I spent a year as an exchange student at [EPFL](https://www.epfl.ch/en/) in Lausanne, Switzerland.

My research sits at the intersection of machine learning and optics. I am especially interested in computational imaging with medical applications. Earlier in my undergraduate studies I also worked on the inverse design of silicon photonic circuits using topology optimization.

Research interests
======
* Computational imaging and inverse problems
* Deep learning for optics: diffusion models, vision-language models, and CNNs
* Imaging through scattering media and holographic reconstruction
* Phase retrieval

Currently
======
I am an undergraduate researcher in the [Mooo Lab](https://mooolab.kaist.ac.kr/index.html) at KAIST, where I lead a project adapting vision-language models to solve imaging problems through scattering media.
